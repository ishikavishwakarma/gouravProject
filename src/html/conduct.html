Code of Conduct

Welcome to the Cloud Village at the Seasides Security Conference! We are dedicated to fostering a safe, inclusive, and respectful environment for all participants. This Code of Conduct outlines our expectations for behavior and the consequences for unacceptable actions.

1. Purpose

The Cloud Village aims to create a collaborative space for individuals interested in cloud security, ethical hacking, and responsible disclosure. We encourage attendees to engage, learn, and contribute positively to the community.

2. Expected Behavior

  • Respect for All: Treat all participants with respect and consideration, valuing diverse perspectives and backgrounds .
  • Collaboration: Engage in supportive communication and work together to enhance learning experiences.
  • Inclusivity: Foster an inclusive atmosphere that welcomes individuals from all backgrounds and skill levels.
  • Constructive Engagement: Focus on critiquing ideas rather than individuals. Provide constructive feedback and graciously accept criticism.
  • Responsible Ethical Conduct: Adhere to ethical hacking practices and respect the rules of the Cloud Village and the Seasides Security Conference.

3. Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behaviors include, but are not limited to:

  • Harassment and Discrimination: Intimidating, harassing, abusive, discriminatory, or derogatory conduct towards any individual or group.
  • Disruption: Disrupting presentations, discussions, or other events.
  • Inappropriate Content: Displaying sexually explicit or violent material.
  • Illegal Activities: Engaging in or promoting illegal behavior, including unauthorized hacking.

4. Consequences of Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior will not be tolerated. Individuals asked to cease such behavior are expected to comply immediately. Consequences for violations may include:

  • Removal from the Village: Participants may be expelled from the Cloud Village at the discretion of the organizers.
  • Banning from Virtual Communities: Offenders may be prohibited from attending future events or participating in related online communities.
  • Banning from Virtual Communities: Serious breaches will be escalated to the Seasides Conference organizers and, if necessary, reported to law enforcement.

5. Reporting Guidelines

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, or have any concerns, please notify a Cloud Village organizer as soon as possible. Organizers can be identified by their badges and will be trained to address and report incidents effectively.

6. Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all participants at the Cloud Village, including attendees, volunteers, organizers, and anyone with an official role. It also extends to related social events and digital spaces associated with the Village.

7. Liability of Information Disclosure

All discussions, presentations, and content shared within the Cloud Village are the responsibility of the individual presenters. Each presenter must ensure they have followed appropriate responsible disclosure processes and obtained necessary consents for any information shared. The Cloud Village organizers and Seasides Conference are not liable for unauthorized disclosures by presenters.

8. Contact Information

For questions or to report a violation of this Code of Conduct, please contact us at support[at]seasidestech.com.

9. General Courtesy

The spirit of the Cloud Village is rooted in community and innovation. Let’s maintain positive and professional interactions — be kind and respectful. Together, we can create a welcoming environment that ensures the Seasides Conference remains a place for everyone.

10. License and Attribution

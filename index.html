Overview

Welcome to the Cloud Security Village at the Seaside Conference, where innovation meets the boundless potential of the cloud—absolutely free of cost! Dive into an exciting world of cutting-edge exploration, hands-on labs, and expert-led sessions without spending a single penny. Whether you're decoding IAM policies, exploring container security, or uncovering vulnerabilities in cloud architectures, there’s a challenge for every skill level. Our mission is to deepen your understanding of securing cloud environments while bridging the gap between cloud practitioners and security researchers. Through engaging challenges and interactive knowledge-sharing sessions, you’ll gain valuable insights into effective defense strategies, real-world attack scenarios, compliance considerations, and best practices for building resilient cloud architectures. Collaborate with fellow cloud enthusiasts, exchange ideas, and master the art of securing the cloud in this dynamic, inclusive, and completely free environment. Let’s secure, hack, and innovate together!
